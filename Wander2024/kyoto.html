Experience Kyoto

The Cultural Heart of Japan

Welcome to Kyoto

Kyoto, the former capital of Japan, is a city of unparalleled beauty and historical significance. Home to over 1,600 Buddhist temples, stunning gardens, and traditional tea houses, Kyoto offers a serene and culturally rich experience for every traveler.

Kyoto Highlights

Cherry Blossoms

Visit Kyoto during the cherry blossom season and witness the beautiful sakura petals falling like snow.

Fushimi Inari Shrine

Walk through the famous red Torii gates of Fushimi Inari, one of the most iconic landmarks in Japan.

Zen Gardens

Explore Kyoto's Zen gardens, where tranquility and nature blend to create peaceful retreats for reflection.
